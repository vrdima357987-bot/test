Unbox the joy of creation!

Our Magic Boxes turn every crafting moment into a fun, hands-on adventure where kids learn, explore, and bring their imagination to life.

Learn more ↗

Small Magic Box

Ideal for beginners and children. It contains everything you need to try sewing without any unnecessary complications.

Contents: a set of cotton, linen and felt fabrics, 0.5 metres of each type, needles and safety pins, thread in three basic colours, small fabric scissors, simple patterns for creating a toy, pillowcase or placemat, including a simple accessory such as a bag, cover or decorative element.
Beginner 0,8 KG

Medium Magic Box

Suitable for those who are already somewhat familiar with sewing and want to make a full-fledged project.

Contents: a set of fabrics made of thick cotton, satin and lining fabric, one metre of each type, fine ribbons of thread, cutting scissors, pins, needles, zipper, buttons or other fastening elements, a pattern with instructions for creating an item of clothing or interior decoration, a little cushion or cosmetic bag.
Intermediate 1,5 KG

Big Magic Box

A premium set for advanced sewers, suitable for creating a complete product with a designer approach.

Contents: an extended set of fabrics (up to seven types), including designer prints and decoration elements, embroideries/stone thread, a tailor's kit with professional scissors, a seam ripper, threads of various types and colours, a wide selection of fasteners, buttons, zips, a complex pattern for making a thing, a full set of accessories, and two PDF instructions. Basic and advanced, as well as QR access to a closed master class from CraftiCo.
Advanced 2,6 KG
