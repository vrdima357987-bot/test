Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction
CraftiCo is a community platform dedicated to crafting, creative expression, and artistic learning. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our services, or participate in our community. This may include your name, email address, payment information, and content you create or share. Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process transactions, send communications, respond to inquiries, maintain platform security, and personalize your experience.

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze platform usage. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

5. Payment Security
CraftiCo.Play and paid services accept both payment cards and cryptocurrency. All transactions are encrypted and processed through secure gateways.
