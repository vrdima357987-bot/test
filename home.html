Design process

1 Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and preferences. During this initial meeting, we discuss your goals, budget, timeline, and any specific requirements you may have. This foundation ensures that every recommendation aligns perfectly with your unique needs and style preferences.

2 Design Development

Based on our consultation, we create detailed design concepts and mood boards that capture your vision. Our team researches the latest trends, sources unique pieces, and develops color palettes that reflect your personality. We present multiple options, allowing you to see different approaches to achieving your dream space.

3 Implementation

Once the design is approved, we handle all aspects of implementation. From sourcing furniture and accessories to coordinating deliveries and installations, we manage every detail. Our network of trusted suppliers and craftspeople ensures high-quality results, while our project management keeps everything on schedule and within budget.
